Stoofvlees van everzwijn

Description

This is a Flemisch recipes and it is heaven

  • wild boar
  • smoked bacon
  • mushrooms
  • onjions
  • red wine
  • flour
  • jam
  • gingerbread
  • mustard

Steps

  1. Cook onjions and bacon
  2. Mix wild boar with the flour and cook
  3. Add muschrooms and onjions/bacon to the wild boar
  4. Add red wine and bring to a simmer
  5. put the mustard and gingerbread on top of the meat
  6. Turn down the heat and mix in the jam
