Chachouka

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Green bell pepper
  • garlic
  • Spices (coriander, cumin, paprika)
  • 2 cans chopped tomatos
  • 6 eggs

Steps

  1. Cook onions, bell peppers and garlic
  2. Add spices
  3. Add tomatoes
  4. Add eggs
  5. Cook until eggs are cookt
  6. Garnish and serve
